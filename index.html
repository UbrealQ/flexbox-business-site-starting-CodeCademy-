Street Art Team

The mission of Positive Street Art is to inspire a passion for the urban arts in a productive way and to build strong communities through educational workshops, community events, and artistic services.

“Art is a universal language. It has no age, no gender, no race. It simply speaks to the hearts and moves humankind in a way nobody can explain, but everybody can experiment. Art makes our world a better place.” Street Art for Mankind
